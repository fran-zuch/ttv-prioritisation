🔭 TTV Target Prioritisation Dashboard

ℹ️ How to Use This Dashboard

This dasboad uses Exoclock data and the targets are ranked using six criteria:

S1 ephemeris quality, S2 TTV signal, S3 instrument suitability (here specifically PIRATE), S4 observability, S5 scientific priority and S6 campaign synergy.

Next, only targets with an observable fraction greater than 0.3 are displayed. This ensures a meaningful fraction of the transit is visible from PIRATE.

Top Targets: Shows the most urgent upcoming observations ranked by score and timing.

Score vs Time Plot: Use to identify high-value, urgent targets (top-right shaded region).

Observability Plot: Identify easiest observations (high visibility, short duration).

Table: Full list of targets (sortable, paginated). Click a row for detailed explanation which is displayed below the table.

Confidence: Combines timing and uncertainty to indicate likelihood of successful follow-up.
