🔭 TTV Target Prioritisation Dashboard

ℹ️ How to Use This Dashboard

This dashboard uses Exoclock data and ranks the targets using six criteria:

S1 Ephemeris Quality (25%), S2 TTV signal (20%), S3 Instrument Suitability (here specifically PIRATE) (15%), S4 Observability (15%), S5 Scientific Priority (15%) and S6 Campaign Synergy (10%).

Note, that only targets with a transit observability greater than 30% and with a minimum Score greater than 3.0 are displayed per default, though these values can be changed by the sliders. This ensures a meaningful fraction of the transit is visible from PIRATE.

Top Targets: Show the most urgent upcoming observations ranked by score and timing. These will change based on the other variables, as will the graphs and table.

Score vs Time Plot: Use to identify high-value, urgent targets (top-right shaded region).

Observability Plot: Identify easiest observations (high visibility, short duration).

Table: Full list of targets (sortable, paginated). Click a row for detailed explanation about the scoring and observational information which is displayed below the table.

Confidence: Combines timing and uncertainty to indicate likelihood of successful follow-up.

The dashboard refreshes daily and will show data for the next 30 days
